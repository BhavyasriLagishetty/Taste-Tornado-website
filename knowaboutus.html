The Founders

L. BHAVYA SRI -Founder of Taste Tornado

L.BHAVYA SRI, the visionary behind Taste Tornado, has always had a passion for culinary arts and the transformative power of food. Growing up in a family that cherished traditional recipes and innovative cooking, Pranathi dreamed of creating a place where flavors from different cultures could converge. Her journey started with a small pop-up kitchen that gained a dedicated following, and with unwavering determination, she transformed this humble beginning into the renowned restaurant, Taste Tornado

"Food is not just about taste; it's an experience that brings people together, tells a story, and creates lasting memories." - L.BHAVYA SRI
